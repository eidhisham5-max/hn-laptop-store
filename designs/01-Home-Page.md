## Tablet (768px - 1023px)

### Layout Adjustments
- Hero section: 2-column layout for text and CTA
- Categories: 2x2 grid instead of 4 columns
- Featured products: 2 columns instead of 4
- Why choose us: 3 columns maintained

## Mobile (320px - 767px)

### Layout Adjustments
- Hero section: Single column, stacked content
- Categories: Single column, stacked cards
- Featured products: Single column, stacked cards
- Why choose us: Single column, stacked cards
- Header: Hamburger menu, simplified navigation

## Interactive States
- **Hover**: Cards lift with shadow, buttons change color
- **Focus**: Clear focus rings for accessibility
- **Loading**: Skeleton screens for content loading
- **Error**: Clear error messages with retry options
